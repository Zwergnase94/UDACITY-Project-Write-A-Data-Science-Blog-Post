Results¶

I wanted to know how HomeOffice affects. For this purpose I worked out 3 questions and one can say that HomeOffize has arrived in today's time. Home office is very well accepted and the number of people who work only from home is even a little more than those who work only in the office. Furthermore, we have found that those who work more at home earn more on average than those who work only in the office. The working hours are similar between those who work at home and those who work in the office. Here we have only looked at three parameters, but there are others that could be taken into consideration.
